EU Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism Takes Effect

The European Union's landmark CBAM policy has officially begun implementation, requiring importers to report carbon emissions for cement, steel, aluminum, electricity, fertilizers and hydrogen. Full carbon pricing will begin in 2026, with experts predicting significant impacts on global trade patterns.
