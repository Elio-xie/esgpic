Climate Solutions

Carbon Removal Technologies: Scaling Challenges and Opportunities

I've just returned from the Carbon Engineering Summit where there was significant focus on direct air capture (DAC) technologies. As these solutions are gaining momentum, I wanted to discuss the key scaling challenges that emerged from the discussions.

Major hurdles include:

  • Energy requirements remain extremely high - most projects still need renewable sources
  • Current cost per ton ($250-600) is prohibitive for wide adoption
  • Storage infrastructure for captured carbon needs significant investment

On the positive side, new catalysts are reducing energy needs by ~18% in lab settings. Anyone here working with the new biomimetic catalysts or have thoughts on storage solutions?

Diversity & Inclusion

Best Practices for Measuring Inclusion Beyond Diversity Metrics

While diversity metrics are important, measuring inclusion and belonging requires different approaches. I recently completed a year-long study with 42 organizations implementing various measurement frameworks.

The most effective metrics we discovered were:

  • Psychological safety scores measured through confidential pulse surveys
  • Decision influence mapping across demographic groups
  • Promotion velocity comparisons adjusted for entry points
  • Meeting equity analytics (speaking time, interruption patterns)

I'm sharing the full report next week, but would love to hear what methods your organizations are finding most insightful for measuring true inclusion beyond the standard diversity stats.
