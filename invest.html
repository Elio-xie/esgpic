Fund Overview

About Global Green Bond Fund

The Global Green Bond Fund invests in a diversified portfolio of fixed income securities, with a focus on investment-grade bonds designated as "green bonds." These bonds specifically fund projects with positive environmental or climate benefits, such as renewable energy infrastructure, energy efficiency initiatives, and clean water projects.

This fund offers investors the opportunity to participate in the transition to a low-carbon economy while earning competitive yields. All bonds in the portfolio undergo rigorous ESG screening to ensure compliance with international green bond standards and principles.

Managed by BlackRock's dedicated sustainable investing team, the fund aims to deliver both financial returns and measurable environmental impact. The portfolio is globally diversified across sectors, regions, and maturities to balance risk and return potential.
